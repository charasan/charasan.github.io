Charasan on Github


A little front page for my stuff here on Github. Based off my site at Charasan.com. The main site itself is little more than a whiteboard for me, so don't expect anything fancier than what you see here.

My projects here on Github:

  • jQuery Audio: This was a very early plugin I wrote, one of my first. More just a test to see if I could write a plugin, from the early days of HTML5.
  • X-Ray Effect: Just a little jQuery/CSS trickery to give the effect of an X-Ray with two images. As the 'window' slides, data points can be revealed as well.
  • Dynamic Thumbnails: Create thumbnails of any size on the fly, and cache the resulting image for later use and quicker load times.
